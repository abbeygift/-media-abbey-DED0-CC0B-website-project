Some Fall Facts!

Besides being one of the seven natural wonders of the world. Victoria Falls is a majestic and breath taking resort town that is perfect for once in a while getaway.

Located at the middle of Zambezi, the waterfall has a 100 meter vertical chasm spanning the full one and a half width of the river. In 1958 the awesome power of the falls recorded the highest flow of 700 000 cubic meters of water per minute.
